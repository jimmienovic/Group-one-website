Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the admission process?
Admission forms are available at the school office. You will need to fill the form and attach the required documents for review.
2. What are the admission requirements?
Those joining S1 and S4 the admission requirement is PLE and UCE slip respectively, Transfer cases in S2, S3, S4 and S6, Report card of the previous school and recommendation from the previous school.
3. What curriculum does Lira Secondary School follow?
We follow the curriculum designed by NCDC and Which is examine by Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) both O-Level and A-Level.
4. Does the school provide boarding facilities?
Yes, we offer only boarding options for our students from S1-S6.
5. How can I contact the school administration?
You can reach us via the contact page or call our office during working hours.
6. What sports and clubs are available?

Sports:

Football, Netball, Basketball, Valleyball, Tennisball, Cricket, Hackey, Handball, Swimming.

Clubs:

Academic Club, Debating Club, ICT Club, Scripture Union Club,Scoutting Club, Music & Dance Club
